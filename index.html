Turn your Receipt into Powerful
Engagement Tool

How it Looks

  • Custom Brand

    Showcase your logo and colors on every receipt for consistent branding.

  • Vouchers

    Embed special offers and discounts directly in the receipt to incentivize repeat purchases.

  • CO2 Emissions

    Detail the carbon footprint for each purchase, promoting eco-awareness.

  • Loyalty

    Integrate loyalty rewards and stamp cards digitally, encouraging repeat visits.

  • Feedback

    Gather immediate customer feedback directly via the receipt for continual improvement.

  • Transaction Data

    See the transaction data, Digitalizing both ESR and payment terminal receipts into one.

How it Works

  • Easy Setup

    Seamlessly integrates with a wide range of POS systems and is compatible with major card providers like Visa and Mastercard, ensuring a quick setup process that only takes a few minutes.

  • Flexible Digital Receipt Option

    Detail the carbon footprint for each purchase, promoting eco-awareness.

  • wCard Linking

    Customers can register their payment cards for automatic digital receipt delivery after each transaction.

  • QR Code

    Customers can scan QR codes from external facing customer displays or payment terminals.

  • NFC

    Customers hold their mobile device close to the payment terminal which will automatically open the digital receipt in the web browser.

  • Fetch Later

    Customers who forgot to take a receipt in the shop and would still need one can fetch a receipt themselves through the web service.

  • Benefits of Going Global

    Eliminate paper receipts, making transactions more eco-friendly & efficient. Customize digital receipts with offers, promotion & loyalty stamps to enhance customer engagement.
